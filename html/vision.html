Vision Mission anfd : Midas Touch is a nongovernmental organization founded in 2009 based at Imphal. It mainly focusses on Biodiversity conservation and we firmly believe in greener earth and better living. It aims to create a sustainable livelihood through community participation while simultaneously conserving the biodiversity. Employment generation of the youth and sustainable living are the foremost goal of our organization. The biodiversity of Manipur comprises endemic floral diversity, particularly medicinal plants of importance to pharmaceutical industry, and unique faunal diversity.{{prologue}}
