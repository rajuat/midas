MIDAS TOUCH’s activities are primarily focused in Imphal West , Imphal East, Bishnupur and Thoubal district of Manipur. Besides, MIDAS TOUCH has also expanded the horizon of its activities and is undertaking numbers of extension programs through different networking with a broad coverage on emerging critical issues surrounding the state which includes the recent disasters caused by the impact of climate changes – earthquakes , flash floods etc. We are also on the process of signing MoU with different like minded organisations so that we can take rapid actions on some of the ongoing problems related to natural calamities and other environmental issues.
